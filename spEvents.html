Special Events

If you are looking for that “WOW” for your next meeting, celebration, product introduction, award ceremony, grand opening or trade show? Look no further, Laser Encore will customize a laser light show specifically for your event! High-tech lasers and special effects can make a powerful statement that will make your event exciting, memorable and stand out.

Our most popular 3D aerial laser beam displays project out over your audience for an amazing powerful effect. These laser displays are all scalable depending on the size of your event, audience and budget.

Projection media using lasers (laser billboards) to re-create your logos, graphics and animations has become popular for corporate events and large scale displays. Great for outdoor advertising on buildings or even sides of mountains.

Lasers can be synchronized to music and video, plus they work great with other effects such as intelligent lighting, pyro, fireworks, flame projectors, fog, confetti and steamers.
